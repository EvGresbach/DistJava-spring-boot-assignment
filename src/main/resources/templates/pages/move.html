Step 3: Movement

The last step in target training your animal is to get them to move around. This may include following the target or having them move from one spot to the (stationary) target. Start small. Get them to move just one or two steps using the command you established in step two. Work up until they are able to follow the target or move to it once they are told. Continue to reward them every time they successfully touch the target.
