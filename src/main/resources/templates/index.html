Target Training Animals

Target training is training an animal to go to a specified target. This type of training has a wide variety of uses once mastered and can be taught to many different species of animals. This is done with a few simple steps: Acknowledgement, touch, and movement.

Targets

Almost anything can be used as a target. This includes a pen/pencil, your hand, a plastic food container lid (for agility), a target stick with a dist or ball at the end, etc. Anything that can be identified by your animal and that can be easily moved.
